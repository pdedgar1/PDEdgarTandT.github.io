    About Me

    P.D. Edgar has an MFA and a master’s in media studies from the University of Alabama, where he was Art & Design Editor of Black Warrior Review. He’s now a Ph.D. student in Texts and Technology at the University of Central Florida, where he studies poetry culture and electronic literature. PD grew up between Managua, Nicaragua, and Central Florida, and is a Freelance Typesetter and Graphic Designer that assists in the publication of graphic novels, literary magazines, and poetry chapbooks. He's also the founding editor of re•mediate, a journal for computer-assisted creative writing.
